Matt's Movie Recommends

My top 5 Movies

2001: A Space Odyssey (1968)

A visually stunning and thought-provoking sci-fi masterpiece about human evolution and the search for extraterrestrial intelligence that explores themes of technology, artificial intelligence, and the nature of consciousness.

The Fall (2005)

A visually captivating and dreamlike film that tells a story within a story by following an injured stuntman who, confined to a hospital bed after an accident, weaves a fantastical tale for a fellow patient, exploring themes of storytelling, imagination, and the power of cinema.

Mr. Nobody (2009)

A mind-bending and philosophical film that explores the concept of free will and the different paths a life can take by following a boy who has to make a decision and then faces the question of whether he made the right choices in his life.

12 Angry Men (1957)

A classic courtroom drama that explores the themes of justice, prejudice, and the power of doubt that follows a jury of twelve men who must deliberate on the guilt or innocence of a young man accused of murder

Rashomon (1950)

A groundbreaking Japanese film that explores the subjective nature of truth and perception that tells the story of a rape and murder through the eyes of four different characters, each of whom offers a conflicting version of events.
